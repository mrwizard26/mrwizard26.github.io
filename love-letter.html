My Dearest Love,

March 2025

Happy 21st Birthday — the day the world got a little brighter because you exist. I wanted this page to feel like a tiny surprise party just for you: soft pastels, playful pompoms, and a heart that keeps promising the same thing every day — I choose you.

From the little things (your laugh over coffee, the way you hum when you're happy) to the moments that already feel like stories we'll tell forever, you turn ordinary days into favorite memories. You teach me patience and courage without ever trying. You celebrate my wins, steady my hands on the hard days, and make even the quiet ones sparkle.

At 21 you are bold and kind and endlessly curious. You carry a warmth that invites strangers to smile and friends to stay. I admire your courage to be yourself and the gentle way you lift others. I'm endlessly proud of the person you are becoming — curious, compassionate, joyfully unapologetic.

Tonight I promise: to keep making you laugh, to make room for your dreams, to cheer you on when you chase them, and to build a life with little rituals that are ours alone. I'll keep collecting silly inside jokes, late-night snacks, and quiet mornings where we just exist together. I'll be your map when you need direction and your anchor when you need rest.

Thank you for choosing me to stand beside you. Thank you for every small kindness and every loud burst of happiness. Thank you for letting me love you in all the goofy, tender ways I do.

Here's to you at 21 — to new adventures, to soft afternoons wrapped in blankets, to PomPom-themed surprises, and to a lifetime of being silly and serious together. I love you more than words on a page can hold, but I'll keep writing them anyway.

All my heart,
